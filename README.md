# Oshili Shield AI

> Empowering Africans to navigate the age of Artificial Intelligence safely.

## Mission

Oshili Shield AI is an AI Trust & Digital Literacy Platform for Africa.

Our mission is to help people understand AI-generated content, misinformation, scams, and emerging digital threats through AI-assisted analysis, education, and accessible learning in African languages.

We do not decide what people should believe.

We help people make better-informed decisions.
